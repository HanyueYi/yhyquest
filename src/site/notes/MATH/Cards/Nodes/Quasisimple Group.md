> [!definition]
> A group is *quasisimple* if it is equal to its commutator subgroup and its inner automorphism group $\mathrm{Inn}(G)\cong G/\mathrm{Z}(G)$ is non-abelian simple. All non-abelian simple groups are quasisimple.
