> [!proposition]
> A $p$-group of order $p^n$ has a normal subgroup of order $p^k$ for each $0 \leq k \leq n$.

`\begin{proof}` 
By induction hypothesis, apply Cauchy theorem to $Z(G/N_k)$ where $N_k$ is normal subgroup of order $p^k$.
`\end{proof}`
